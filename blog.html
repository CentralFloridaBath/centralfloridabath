5 Tips to Prepare for a Shower/Bath Remodel

Posted: Feb 2025 • Read time: 3 min

1) Set a realistic budget. Include a small buffer for surprises. 2) Decide your must-haves vs. nice-to-haves. 3) Choose materials early. Lead times can affect schedules. 4) Before the installers arrive, clear the space. Remove décor, toiletries, and rugs. 5) Plan for dust and access. Keep a path clear and protect floors.
